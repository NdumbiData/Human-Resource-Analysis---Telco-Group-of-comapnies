# Workforce Performance and Sales Optimization Using Data-Driven Strategies  

##  Project Overview  
This project applies data analytics, machine learning, and financial analysis to optimize workforce performance and sales operations for Tecos Group of Companies. By leveraging HR and sales data, we develop predictive models and generate insights to improve revenue and workforce efficiency.  

##  About the Company  
Tecos Group of Companies is a multinational retailer headquartered in Welwyn City, England. Despite a growing customer base, the company faces challenges in:  
- Achieving predictable revenue growth.  
- Enhancing workforce productivity and reducing turnover.  

Our goal is to provide data-driven strategies to overcome these challenges.  

##  Key Objectives  
- Develop predictive models for sales forecasting.  
- Analyze workforce performance metrics to identify productivity gaps.  
- Evaluate financial implications of workforce and sales initiatives.  
- Provide actionable recommendations for revenue growth and employee retention.  
- Present insights through interactive dashboards and reports.  

##  Data Sources  
The project utilizes real-world datasets, including:  
- **HR Dataset:** Employee details, performance metrics, absenteeism, and turnover trends.  
- **Sales Dataset:** Transaction data, regional sales trends, and product demand patterns.  

##  Tools & Technologies  
- **Programming:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn)  
- **Machine Learning:** Regression models, time series forecasting  
- **Data Visualization:** Power BI, Tableau  
- **Project Management:** Agile methodologies  

##  Expected Outcomes  
- Improved sales predictions through data-driven insights.  
- Enhanced workforce productivity and retention strategies.  
- Actionable financial evaluations for business decision-making.
